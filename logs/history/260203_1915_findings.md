# Highlights: TODOs Location Clarification

**Date**: 2026-02-03 19:15
**Context**: Update 2 to ref/setup.md - clarifying location of TODOs and decisions in journals

---

## Issue Identified

In `journals/2026_02_02.md`, 4 blocks were found loose at the end of the journal:
- 1 `#decision` block
- 1 `DOING` block
- 3 `TODO` blocks

Root cause: `ref/setup.md` lacked explicit guidelines on where TODOs and decisions should be located. Only said "Create TODO block in Journal" but didn't specify if inside categories or loose at the end.

---

## Solution Implemented

**Decision**: TODOs and #decision must be **inside thematic categories**.

**Justification**:
1. Immediate context: block is adjacent to related content
2. No duplication: no need to maintain two locations
3. Query compatible: Logseq captures all blocks with `project:: [[CALAIRE-EA]]` regardless of location
4. Consistency: same pattern as narrative content

---

## Changes Made

### 1. ref/setup.md

Added section "Ubicación de TODOs y Decisiones" (lines ~111-122):

```markdown
### Ubicación de TODOs y Decisiones

- **TODOs** y **#decision** van **dentro de la categoría temática** correspondiente.
- No crear secciones separadas para tareas al final del journal.
- Esto mantiene contexto inmediato y evita duplicación.

| Tipo de bloque | Ubicación |
|----------------|-----------|
| `TODO`, `DOING`, `DONE` | Bajo la categoría temática donde aplica |
| `#decision` | Bajo la categoría donde aplica la decisión |
```

## Pattern Established

**Rule**: All blocks (narrative, TODO, DOING, DONE, #decision) must be placed under their thematic category in daily journals.

**Exception**: Meeting pages (`#[[Reunión: ...]]`) have their own structure but TODOs inside meetings are correct.

**Historical Notes**: Journals marked as `#nota-historica` do not require categories (by design).
